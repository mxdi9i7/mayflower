mayflower wyndham hotel


Officially opened for business on March 28, 2017, Wyndham Garden Fresh Meadows Flushing Hotel covers an area of 12,000 square feet, 12 floors and 150 rooms. The hotel is located in Queens and 5 km from Arthur Ashe Stadium. Guest can enjoy the bar, private parking and free Wi-Fi at this hotel.

Some guestrooms have a seating area where you can relax. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a bathin the private bathroom, as well as free toiletries and a hair dryer. Wyndham Garden Fresh Meadows Flushing features 2 meeting rooms.

Wyndham Garden Fresh Meadows Flushing Hotel is 5 km from Citi Field, 7 km from Belmont Park Race Track, and 8 km from LaGuardia Airport.
