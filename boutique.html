mayflower boutique hotel


Located on Long Island City, New York, Mayflower Boutique hotel covers an area of 2975 square feet (9 floors above ground, 1 basement, 50 rooms), a 1491-square-foot restaurant, an open kitchen, a small office, lounge, Laundry room and storage room on the basement,; on the first floor, there is the reception lobby and three accessible rooms; on the seventh floor there is a 740-square -foot rooftop.

Mayflower Boutique Hotel is 1.9 km from Gantry Plaza State Park. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi. Located in Long Island City, Queens, the hotel is 8 km from Citi Field, and 8 km from Arthur Ashe Stadium. A 24-hour front desk and luggage storage are available.

All rooms at the Mayflower Boutique Hotel have a seating area. The private bathroom has a bath or shower and free toiletries. All rooms at hotel feature a flat-screen TV and a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Mayflower Boutique Hotel is 13 km from the Aqueduct Racetrack, 19 km from the Belmont Park Race Track, and 6 km away from LaGuardia Airport.
