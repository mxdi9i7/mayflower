mayflowers hotel wuhan


Mayflowers Hotel Wuhan is located in Wuchang CBD, the hotel adjoins business center of Zhongnan business area, where there're municipal government, universities and colleges, research institutes, Chuhehanjie Street, the Museum of Wuchang Uprising of 1911 Revolution, East lake High-Tech Development Zone. Look out into the distance from the upper floors window of the hotel, guests may get a panoramic view of Yellow Crane Tower, Snake-shaped Mountain, Taoist temple Changchunguan and other beautiful tourist attractions. Geographically, the hotel enjoys traffic convenience: it takes only 5-minute walk to metro railway station, 5-minute drive to Yellow Crane Tower and Yangtze River Bund. The driving distance to WuchangRailway Station, Wuhan Railway Station and Wuhan Tianhe International Airport is 5-minute, 40-minute, and 50-minute respectively. Mayflowers Hotel Wuhan, with updated accommodation facilities and considerate services, let you feel the warmth of home.

With a building area of 32,000 square meters and a 26-storey building, the hotel has 286 guestrooms, including 50 suites and presidential suites.

The hotel is designed by Hong Kong's famous G.I.L Design Company. From the construction to the decoration, the hotel focuses on details and reflects human oriented design everywhere.
